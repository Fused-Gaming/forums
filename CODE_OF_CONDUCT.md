# FUSED GAMING Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful
- Treat all community members with respect and consideration
- Assume good intentions; approach disagreements constructively
- Provide constructive feedback, not personal attacks

### Be Helpful
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Answer questions patiently, especially for newcomers
- Acknowledge when you don't know something

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions on-topic and relevant
- Use appropriate language; avoid excessive profanity
- Respect privacy; don't share others' personal information

### Be Honest
- Don't spread misinformation or FUD
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant
- Report bugs and vulnerabilities responsibly

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- **Harassment**: Including offensive comments, deliberate intimidation, stalking, or unwanted attention
- **Discrimination**: Based on any protected characteristic
- **Spam**: Unsolicited promotions, referral links, or repetitive content
- **Scams**: Phishing attempts, fake giveaways, or fraudulent schemes
- **Doxxing**: Publishing others' private information without consent
- **Abuse**: Personal attacks, threats, or violent language
- **Disruption**: Intentionally derailing discussions or trolling

## Security & Safety

### Wallet Safety
- NEVER share your private keys or seed phrases
- Team members will NEVER ask for your private keys
- Be skeptical of DMs offering "support" or "airdrops"

### Responsible Disclosure
If you discover a security vulnerability:
1. Do NOT post publicly
2. Email security@vln.gg with details
3. Allow reasonable time for remediation
4. See our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) for bounty information

## Enforcement

### Reporting
Report violations by:
- Using GitHub's built-in report feature
- Emailing conduct@fusedgaming.com
- Contacting a moderator directly

All reports are reviewed confidentially.

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning**: First-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Ban**: Repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent Ban**: Severe violations or continued misbehavior

### Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error:
- Email appeals@fusedgaming.com
- Include relevant context and your perspective
- Appeals are reviewed by senior team members

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub Discussions, Issues, and PRs
- Discord and other official community spaces
- Public spaces when representing FUSED GAMING
- Direct interactions with community members
